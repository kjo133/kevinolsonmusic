KEVIN OLSON

Hello! I'm a musician from the Washington DC area. I am best known for playing my percussive acoustic guitar at basketball games. Have performed at primetime games and tournaments throughout the United States.

Locally, I play at music venues throughout the DC, Northern Virginia, and Maryland areas both solo and with my band, The Sprinting Turtles. Send me a message to collaborate, book a show, or to just say 'hi!'!
